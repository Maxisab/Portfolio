About Me

Hello there! I'm Max, a passionate and creative Web Developer with a keen eye for detail and a love for crafting high-quality, user-centric digital experiences. I have over 1 year of hands-on experience with my own company. I really enjoy working with people to turn their ideas into reality through code and design.


I've always been fascinated by tech and remember dipping my toes into coding HTML back in the MySpace days. Since then I didn't go too much further until two years ago when I started teaching myself to code using the internet and other friends in the field. It has been a fun journey developing my skill set and I feel ready to take on the next step.


I believe what sets me apart from other developers is my ability to collaborate with others, communicate effectively, and my ability to adapt to new technologies and trends. I'm a very quick learner and adept at combining new technologies and methodologies efficiently. This has allowed me to adapt to new languages and frameworks as needed, either by my clients or for my own needs.


When I'm not coding you can find me playing drums with my band, playing board games with friends or exploring the beautiful PNW on my motorcycle. I believe that having passions and hobbies outside of work really contribute to a more well rounded work life and also help with critical and creative thinking.


If you're looking for someone who is dedicated, adaptive, and versatile I'd love to connect and discuss how I can help bring your ideas to life. I look forward to hearing from you!
